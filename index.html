Frequently asked

How do I play Who's in the Box?

A new mystery big-league player steps into the box each day. You have 9 guesses. Every guess colors five clue columns — Team, Division, Position, Arm, and Debut — green (match), yellow (close), or red (miss). Read the colors to narrow down who it is.

Does everyone get the same player?

Yes. The daily mystery player is the same for every player on a given calendar day, so you can compare results with friends.

What is Rundown?

Rundown is a fast, timed survival mode. A mystery player reveals one clue at a time — call the right card before the clock runs out. Fewer clues mean bigger points. Stack combos and climb as many levels as you can.

Is my streak saved?

Your streak and game history are stored on your device. There's no account or login required to play.

The rosters look out of date.

SignSteal uses real big-league rosters that refresh through the season. If something looks off, email us the details and we'll take a look.
